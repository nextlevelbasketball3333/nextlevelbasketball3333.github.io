Next Level Mindset

It's more than the training itself. It the bigger picture of what you want with your goals. We are a goal driven program where we want you to reach your goals and once you reached them, we will set new ones and higher ones.

This is our home

We are based in Thao Dien, District 2 in HCMC. This is a home built from our own hands and we have made sure that we have everything we need for our players to only focus on Basketball.

Our mission

This is a place by players for players. We want to create a home for everyone from the grassroots level to professionals under the same roof. Next Level. We train you at any level. From beginners to professionals. We want to make sure that you have the best environment for basketball. That is why our equipment are top of the line from Dr. Dish Shooting Machine, Normatec 2.0 Recovery, and a full-sized weight lifting gym to improve your physicals that translates to the basketball court.
