Meet The Team

Basketball Experts

Stefan Nguyen
Founder
My name is Stefan Nguyen and I've been a professional basketball player since I graduated 2011 from high school at Luleå Riksbasket gymnasium which is one of two national sports high school institutes in Sweden. I have been playing professional basketball combined with my university studies at Malmö University and graduated 2015. I have been playing professionally both in SwedenEurope and with Saigon Heat since 2015. 2016 was my first year playing in Vietnam Basketball Association for Da Nang Dragons. Then moved on to Hanoi Buffaloes.

I have played two SEA Games for Vietnam National Team. 2017 & 2019 which on the last one ended in Vietnam's first ever medal in Basketball. I am currently coaching Da Nang Dragons in VBA along with Head Coach Phan Thanh Canh.

This is my opportunity for me to pass on my knowledge to the next generation coming up. As of right now, I am rehabilitating an injury and therefore this opportunity to coach has opened up. Being in different environments from amateur, student athlete and professional have helped me a lot and I gained experience from every aspect of the game. Therefore I believe that I can not only teach but also relate with you as an individual of being in that stage of life.

We will work on details that you normally don't think of, which is very important if you want to take your game to The Next Level.
Coach Jungsoo
Assistant Coach

Coach Jungsoo is our head coach of the fundamental groups and youth section. He is USA Youth Basketball Licensed and has coached at Next Level since 2020. He is coaching everyone from VBA Professionals, D1 Athletes to you. He has been working with Samsung Thunders of Korean Basketball League and a stint with Da Nang Dragons on skills training with the imports and locals.

"Coach Jungsoo is the most patient and understanding coach I´ve ever worked with. That is also why he is handling our youngest groups. He is a favorite amongst our younger groups with his personality."
